What is 2FA and why should I use it?
Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your online accounts. Even if someone steals your password, they cannot access your account without the dynamic verification code generated by this app.
How do I add a new account?
You can add accounts in two ways:
  • Scan QR Code: Tap the "+" button and scan the QR code provided by the service (e.g., Google, Facebook).
  • Manual Entry: If you cannot scan the code, select "Manual Entry" and paste the secret key provided by the service.
Do I need an internet connection?
No. The codes are generated locally on your device based on a time algorithm. The app works perfectly in Airplane Mode or offline.

If I lose my phone, can I recover my codes?
This depends on your backup settings:
Important: If you have enabled iCloud Sync/Backup within the app, your data is safe. Simply log in with the same Apple ID on a new device to restore it.

If you did not enable backup, the keys are stored only on your device's chip for security. In this case, data cannot be recovered. We strongly recommend enabling cloud backup or saving the "Recovery Codes" provided by your service providers.

How do I transfer to a new iPhone?
1. Ensure iCloud Sync is enabled on your old device.
2. Sign in to your new iPhone with the same Apple ID.
3. Download the app, and your accounts will appear automatically.

Do you store my passwords or keys?
Absolutely not. We use a Zero-Knowledge architecture. Your secret keys are encrypted and stored locally on your device (in the iOS Keychain) or in your personal iCloud. We (the developers) have no access to your data.
Can I lock the app?
Yes. You can enable Face ID / Touch ID in the Settings. This ensures that only you can view your verification codes.

Why is my code saying "Invalid" or "Incorrect"?
The most common reason is that your device's Time is not perfectly synced. TOTP codes require precise timing.

Fix: Go to iPhone Settings > General > Date & Time, and ensure "Set Automatically" is turned ON.
